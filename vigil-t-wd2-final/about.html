Our Story


Black Coupe Collectibles got it's start way back in 1945 with just one classic car. Earlglen Denford and his wife Lucy bought their first ever car. Every Saturday, Earlglen would take Lucy shopping in the North Dakota countryside for collectibles and antiques, loading up the back of their shiny black coupe. Ever since, the Denford Family passion for collecting the finest quality antiques, heirlooms and fine furnishings in the northwestern Unites States has carried on through four generations.
